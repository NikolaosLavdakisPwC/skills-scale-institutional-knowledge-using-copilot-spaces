# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies, support team health, and help remove impediments. They coach the team on agile practices and ensure the process runs smoothly without becoming a bottleneck.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups, sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives
- Remove or escalate blockers and impediments
- Coach team members on agile practices and self-organization
- Shield the team from distractions and unplanned work
- Track team velocity and help improve sprint predictability
- Collaborate with Project Manager to align delivery practices

### Goals
- Maximize team throughput and predictability
- Foster continuous improvement and team ownership
- Minimize friction in delivery process

### Typical Communication
- Facilitation during sprint ceremonies
- Blocker escalation to PM or technical leads
- Metrics reports (velocity, burndown, cycle time)
- Retrospective action items and follow-ups

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **Project Manager**: Coordinates on timelines, risk management, and cross-team dependencies
- **Product Manager**: Ensures backlog is refined and ready for sprint planning
- **Developers**: Daily support to identify and resolve blockers
- **DevOps Engineer**: Collaborates on automation improvements and CI/CD friction points

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers are responsible for user experience strategy, creating user flows, wireframes, and prototypes. They ensure products are intuitive, accessible, and meet usability goals through research and testing.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Define user flows and information architecture
- Collaborate with Product Manager on requirements and acceptance criteria
- Work with Developers to ensure design implementation fidelity
- Maintain design system and component libraries

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive and accessible user experiences
- Validate designs through user feedback and testing
- Ensure consistency across product features

### Typical Communication
- Design critiques and reviews with product and engineering
- User research findings and usability test reports
- Handoff specifications and design assets to developers
- Feedback sessions during sprint reviews

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **Product Manager**: Collaborates on feature requirements and user needs
- **Business Analyst**: Coordinates on user stories and acceptance criteria
- **Developers**: Provides design specifications and reviews implementation
- **Technical Writer**: Aligns on user-facing language and help documentation

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers own automation, CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure, and deployment processes. They enable rapid, reliable releases and maintain observability for production systems.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure provisioning and configuration
- Implement monitoring, alerting, and observability tools
- Automate deployment and rollback procedures
- Ensure security scanning and compliance in pipelines
- Support incident response and system reliability

### Goals
- Enable fast, safe deployments with minimal manual intervention
- Maintain high system availability and performance
- Reduce mean time to recovery (MTTR) for incidents

### Typical Communication
- Deployment notifications and release windows
- Infrastructure changes and maintenance schedules
- Incident reports and post-mortems
- CI/CD improvement proposals

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Provides tooling and pipeline support for builds and deployments
- **Project Manager**: Coordinates on deployment schedules and production readiness
- **Scrum Master**: Collaborates on removing automation and tooling impediments
- **Technical Writer**: Documents deployment procedures and runbooks

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge the gap between business stakeholders and technical teams. They elicit requirements, document business processes, and ensure solutions meet business needs.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document business requirements
- Translate business needs into user stories and acceptance criteria
- Create process flows and requirement specifications
- Facilitate workshops with stakeholders to clarify needs
- Validate that delivered features meet business objectives
- Identify opportunities for process improvement

### Goals
- Ensure clear, unambiguous requirements
- Maximize alignment between business needs and technical solutions
- Reduce rework by validating requirements early

### Typical Communication
- Requirements documents and user story specifications
- Stakeholder interviews and workshop facilitation
- Acceptance criteria reviews with Product Manager and Developers
- Business process documentation

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **Product Manager**: Collaborates on feature prioritization and business value
- **UX Designer**: Coordinates on user needs and experience requirements
- **Developers**: Clarifies requirements and validates implementation
- **Project Manager**: Provides input on scope and timeline estimates

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers maintain internal and external documentation, ensuring clarity, accuracy, and accessibility. They support team communication and user-facing content.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain user-facing documentation (guides, tutorials, API docs)
- Document internal processes, runbooks, and troubleshooting guides
- Ensure documentation standards and consistency
- Support onboarding materials for new team members
- Review technical content for clarity and accessibility
- Collaborate with teams to capture knowledge and best practices

### Goals
- Provide clear, accurate, and up-to-date documentation
- Reduce support burden through self-service resources
- Improve team knowledge sharing and onboarding efficiency

### Typical Communication
- Documentation reviews and content drafts
- Style guide updates and standards documentation
- Collaboration on release notes and changelogs
- Onboarding guides and training materials

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Collaborates on technical accuracy of documentation
- **UX Designer**: Aligns on user-facing language and help content
- **DevOps Engineer**: Documents deployment procedures and infrastructure
- **Product Manager**: Creates feature documentation and release notes
